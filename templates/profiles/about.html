Born From a Personal Struggle

BdDoc24 was born from a simple yet powerful realization: finding the right doctor in Bangladesh shouldn't be a challenge. Our founders experienced firsthand the frustration of navigating fragmented healthcare information and unreliable referrals during a medical emergency.

What started as a personal quest has evolved into a platform built on the pillars of transparency, verification, and accessibility. We are dedicated to bridging the gap between patients and healthcare professionals, connecting millions of Bangladeshis to the care they deserve.

Trusted by 10,000+ happy patients

Our Mission

Help every person in Bangladesh choose the right healthcare by offering a complete, trusted, and easy to use doctor directory that removes confusion from finding the right doctor

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and indispensable digital health resource in Bangladesh, fostering a healthier nation by seamlessly connecting patients with the right care at the right time.

How We Ensure Accurate Information

BdDoc24 compiles doctor listings from trusted sources such as hospital websites, publicly available medical records (such as BMDC listings). Each listing is reviewed to reduce errors and keep information consistent.

We regularly update data and allow users to report inaccuracies. While we strive for accuracy, we recommend confirming important details directly with the healthcare provider.
