Platform Policy

Editorial Policy

BdDoc24 is a doctor listing platform dedicated to helping people across Bangladesh find verified healthcare professionals easily and reliably.

1. Our Mission

Our mission is to make healthcare access easier by connecting patients with qualified doctors across Bangladesh. BdDoc24 helps users discover doctors, clinics, and hospitals based on specialization, location, and availability.

2. Nature of Our Platform

BdDoc24 is not a medical content or advice platform. We do not publish articles, treatment guides, or medical recommendations.

1

Doctor Listings

We provide structured doctor profiles including name, qualifications, specialization, chamber details, and contact information.

2

Hospital & Clinic Info

Users can find information about hospitals, diagnostic centers, and clinics where doctors provide services.

3

No Medical Advice

We do not provide diagnosis, prescriptions, or treatment recommendations. All medical decisions should be made in consultation with a licensed doctor.

3. Data Accuracy & Verification

We strive to keep all doctor information accurate and up to date by:

Verified Information

Collecting publicly available professional details and verifying credentials where possible.

Regular Updates

Updating chamber schedules, locations, and contact details periodically.

4. Independence & Fairness

BdDoc24 maintains a neutral and transparent listing system.

  • No Ranking Manipulation: Doctors cannot pay to unfairly influence their ranking or visibility.
  • Clear Sponsored Listings: Any promoted listings are clearly marked for transparency.

Correction Policy

If any doctor information is incorrect or outdated, we encourage users and providers to report it.

We review and update listings as quickly as possible to ensure accuracy.

5. Platform Limitations

BdDoc24 is a directory platform only. We do not:

  • • Provide medical advice or treatment
  • • Guarantee doctor availability or outcomes
  • • Replace direct consultation with healthcare professionals
