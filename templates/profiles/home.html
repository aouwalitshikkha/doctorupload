Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using BdDoc24

How do I search for doctors on BdDoc24?
Use the search bar at the top of the page. Make sure "Doctor" tab is selected, then enter the doctor's name, specialty, or any keyword. You can also filter by location and specialty from the doctor list page.
How can I find doctors by location?
You can filter doctors by location using the search filters on the doctor list page. Select your preferred city or area to see doctors available in that location. You can also search for doctors and hospitals directly in your area.
How do I search for hospitals?
Click on the "Hospital" tab in the search bar at the top of the page. Enter the hospital name or location to find healthcare facilities. You can also browse all hospitals from the "All Hospitals" section on the home page.
How do I book an appointment with a doctor?
Click on any doctor's profile to view their details, qualifications, and contact information. Call the hospital directly using the phone numbers provided to schedule an appointment.
What information can I find on doctor profiles?
Each doctor profile includes their qualifications, specialties, years of experience, hospital affiliation, location, contact information, and professional experience. You can also see related doctors and frequently asked questions about that doctor.
How do I contact a hospital?
Visit the hospital's detail page to find contact numbers, address, and other information. You can also find hospital contact numbers on any doctor's profile page who works at that hospital in the "Hospital Contact" section.
Is it free to use BdDoc24?
Yes, BdDoc24 is completely free for patients. You can search for doctors and hospitals, view their profiles, and book appointments without any charges. Our platform helps connect patients with healthcare providers across Bangladesh.
How do I register as a healthcare provider?
If you're a doctor or hospital administrator, click on "Register as Doctor" or "Register Hospital" button in the CTA section on our home page. You can also visit our contact page to send us a registration request and our team will get in touch with you.
