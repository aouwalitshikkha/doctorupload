Legal & Privacy

Privacy Policy

BdDoc24 respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and how third-party services may process information.

1. Introduction

BdDoc24 is a doctor directory platform that helps users find healthcare professionals across Bangladesh. We do not require user registration and do not collect personal or medical information directly.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

BdDoc24 does not collect or store:

  • • Personal identification information (name, phone, email)
  • • Medical or health-related data
  • • User accounts or login details

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services that may collect and process data automatically:

  • Google Analytics: Tracks website usage and visitor behavior.
  • Google Search Console: Helps monitor website performance in search results.
  • Google AdSense: Displays advertisements and may use cookies for personalized ads.
  • Ezoic: Optimizes ads and user experience, and may collect usage data.

These services may use cookies, IP addresses, and device information according to their own privacy policies.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Third-party services may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze traffic, and serve advertisements. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Responsibility

BdDoc24 does not directly collect user data. However, third-party providers may collect information independently. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of these services for more details.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
