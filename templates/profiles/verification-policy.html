Trust & Safety

Doctor Verification Policy

BdDoc24 uses trusted public and official sources to ensure that listed doctors are accurate, reliable, and professionally recognized.

1. Our Approach to Verification

BdDoc24 is a doctor listing platform that relies on publicly available and official information to verify healthcare professionals. Our goal is to provide users with accurate and trustworthy doctor profiles across Bangladesh.

2. Verification Sources

1

BMDC Registration

We use the Bangladesh Medical and Dental Council (BMDC) registration number, where available, to confirm that a doctor is officially registered and licensed to practice.

2

Hospital & Clinic Websites

We collect and cross-check doctor information from official hospital and clinic websites where doctors are listed as practicing consultants or specialists.

3

Public & Professional Sources

Additional information may be gathered from publicly available professional profiles, medical directories, and institutional listings to ensure completeness.

3. Accuracy & Limitations

While we strive to ensure accuracy, BdDoc24 depends on external and publicly available data sources. Doctor availability, chamber schedules, or affiliations may change without notice.

Users are encouraged to verify details directly with the hospital or clinic before visiting.

4. Continuous Updates

We regularly review and update doctor profiles to reflect the most recent information available from our sources. However, updates may not always be immediate.

Report Incorrect Information

If you find incorrect or outdated doctor information, please report it to us. We will review and update it as quickly as possible.
